Revolutionary FAR Overhaul Status

Revolutionary FAR Overhaul

Under Executive Order 14275, Restoring Common Sense to Federal Procurement Executive Order, a comprehensive overhaul of the FAR is being completed. The Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO) website is the primary source for related information. Agencies issued class deviations to direct their personnel to use the RFO parts in advance of the Code of Federal Regulations and acquistion.gov updates. The Defense Pricing, Contracting, and Acquisition Policy (DPCAP) team created a dedicated page for the DFARS Revolutionary FAR Overhaul Class Deviations.
