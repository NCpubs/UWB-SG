Competition

The Competition in Contracting Act (CICA) and FAR 6.101 require, with certain limited exceptions, that contracting officers promote and provide for full and open competition. [!] Consider (1) duplication of costs and (2) unacceptable delay.

Justification and Approvals (J&As)

*J&As must be coordinated with the PEO/Head of Procuring Activity and the HCA prior to SAF/AQ approval.
Changes After J&A Approval: (1) Outside Scope=New J&A; (2) In Scope with $ > J&A Authority=Amended J&A; (3) In scope within J&A Authority=No new/amended J&A is required. [DAFFARS 5306.304(a)].
The justifications shall be made publicly available within 14 days after the award unless the award is made using 6.302-2 (30 days after the award) or is a brand name justification (posted with the solicitation). Must be posted for 30 days.

Exceptions to Full and Open Competition

Mnemonic

Competition wasn't necessary because ...

One Unusual Impala Ate All New Products.

FAR 6.302 establishes the circumstances permitting other than full and open competition.

1

Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services will Satisfy Agency Requirements (or to limit sources);

2

Unusual and Compelling Urgency (Note: J&A can be approved after the award. Only fulfill urgent needs.);

3

Industrial Mobilization, Engineering, Developmental or Research Capability; or Expert Services;

4

International Agreement

5

Authorized or Required by Statute

6

National Security

7

Public Interest
